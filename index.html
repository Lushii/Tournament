INST

International Natural Sciences Tournament

Chemistry. Physics. Biology. Pharmaceuticals. Engineering

INST is an active and challenging competition in applied Chemistry, Physics, Pharmaceuticals, Biology and Engineering for the young bright scientists.

I can participate in INST as:

Team member

Bachelor and master undergraduate students.

Coach

PhD students, holders and stuff in related fields.

Intramural round

Tournament dates: October 11 – 16, 2016

  • 1.Present solutions to the experts

  • 2.Discuss Solutions with colleagues and experts

  • 3.Win:)
