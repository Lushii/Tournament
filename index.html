  • 2010

    First International Students Chemical Tournament was held at St. Petersburg State University, Russia. It was attended by the teams from Russia and Ukraine. The official language of the competition was Russian.

  • 2011

    Second International Natural Sciences Tournament was also held at St. Petersburg State University Russia. The number of the teams grew up. Problems on Biology and Physics were added to the competition. The official language of the competition was Russian.

  • 2012

    Third International Natural Sciences Tournament is best remembered for the claim of presentation one of the tasks in English. Problems on Pharmaceuticals were added to the competition.

  • 2013

    Fourth International Natural Sciences Tournament again surprised the participants stating a full day competition in English during the second day of Qualifying Games. Problems on Engineering were added to the competition.

  • 2014

    Fifth International Natural Sciences Tournament was divided into two separate leagues: Russian-speaking and English-speaking (International) once. The tournament was attended by the teams from Russia, Belarus, India and Indonesia.

  • 2015

    Sixth International Natural Sciences Tournament was also held in to separate leagues: Russian-speaking and International once. More than 20 teams from 18 countries and four continents solved the tasks in the extramural round.

  • 2016

    Seventh International Natural Sciences Tournament is moving to Novosibirsk State University (Russia). The official language of the competition will be English. We expect to grow up the number of the teams.

  • 2017

    Eighth International Natural Sciences Tournament will take place in a beautiful city Agra, India. The official language of the competition will be English.
